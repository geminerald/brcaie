Our History

BRCA Ireland is a voluntary organisation set up in 2020 by a group of women with BRCA 1 and BRCA 2 gene alterations to improve care being provided to those affected in Ireland.

Our Goals

    We undertake:
  1. 1. To raise awareness about BRCA 1 and 2 gene alterations
  2. 2. To work with patient organisations, researchers, healthcare professionals and governmental agencies to support BRCA 1 and BRCA 2 patients and families
  3. 3. To provide advocacy that shapes government policy and to campaign for improvements in care for people and families with BRCA 1 and BRCA 2
  4. 4. To support research that enhances care for people and families with BRCA 1 and BRCA 2
  5. 5. To provide updates on developments in the BRCA 1 and BRCA 2 through our website
